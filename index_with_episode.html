The Invisible One – Episode 1

Sometimes I wonder if I was born to be a shadow. I’m Adaeze. The third daughter of Chief and Lolo Nwankwo. And in this house... I don’t shine.



My elder sister, Amaka, is a lawyer — bold, beautiful, the kind that turns heads at weddings. Then there’s Ifunanya — our family’s “nurse with a heart of gold.”



When people see them, they smile. They remember. They ask questions. But me? I’m the girl they always forget to introduce.



Once, during a family event, someone asked my mother how many children she had. She said, “Two daughters and one son.”



I was standing right beside her.
